# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple principle:

> Build with ambition. Operate with respect.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, inclusive, and constructive.
- Provide helpful and actionable feedback.
- Assume positive intent, but protect the ecosystem from abuse.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Hate speech or inflammatory language.
- Any form of abuse, spam, or malicious activity.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct,
please contact the project maintainers through the appropriate private channels.

The maintainers reserve the right to take appropriate action, including
restricting access or contributions, to protect the health of the project
and its community.
